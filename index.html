Goat Orders is an online platform that specializes in delivering high-quality goat meat in Zimbabwe. We offer various goat meat cuts, including diced, chops, shanks, leg, and offal, with options for home delivery. Established in 2019, our goal is to become the largest supplier of goat meat in Zimbabwe and beyond.

For more, .visit Goat Orders

Goat Goolash is a Zimbabwean gourmet brand specializing in unique goat-based dishes, including burgers, burritos, wraps, and kebabs.

We emphasize flavor, freshness, and convenience, with offerings like goat bacon cheeseburgers, goat liver wraps, and goat guacamole burritos. The company also provides a delivery service, with free delivery for orders over $15.

Our menu combines various creative recipes, all focused on delivering rich and bold flavors.

For more, .visit Goat Orders

GoatPay is a digital financial technology platform by Goat Orders Co., designed to revolutionize goat pastoralism in Zimbabwe. It leverages AI, blockchain, cloud computing, and big data to enhance goat production, investment, and value addition.

The platform connects smallholder goat farmers, buyers, investors, and service providers in a digital marketplace, offering tools to improve goat farming's profitability.

Through blockchain-based smart contracts, GoatPay facilitates transparent, fair transactions and provides financial inclusion solutions, enabling farmers to access credit, insurance, and savings to boost productivity and income

NxtGenGoat is a next-generation innovation aimed at addressing the challenges faced by smallholder goat farmers in Zimbabwe. It focuses on reinventing goat pastoralism by incorporating technology into goat farming to promote fairness, transparency, and increased profitability.

he initiative includes the use of a digital marketplace that connects goat farmers, buyers, investors, and service providers, allowing for the tokenization and transaction of goats. The platform helps farmers optimize production, improve market access, and gain technical and economic data to make informed decisions about their goat farming practices.
